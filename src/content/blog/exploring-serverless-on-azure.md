Serverless computing is changing how we build and deploy applications. By removing the need to manage infrastructure, developers can focus entirely on the **Technical Outcome**.

## Problem
Traditional infrastructure management slows down innovation. Scaling and maintenance become bottlenecks for small teams.

## Decision
Choosing Azure Functions allows for event-driven scaling and a pay-as-you-go model, which fits the "High Speed" experimentation mindset of the Technical Outcome Leader.

## Learning
Serverless isn't just about cost; it's about **Time-to-Value**. By reducing friction, we shape the world of tomorrow faster.
