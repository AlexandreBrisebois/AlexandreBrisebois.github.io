# Branding System: srvrlss.dev

## Persona: Technical Outcome Leader
The bridge between deep engineering expertise (Microsoft ecosystem) and high-speed innovation (AI, 3D Printing, Cloud).

## [EDITOR] Protocol (Clarity & Speed)
- **The 8th Grade Rule**: Explain complex cloud concepts with simple words.
- **Active Voice**: Always use [Subject] [Verb] [Object].
- **Banned Words**: Delete "Utilize," "Leverage," "Deep-dive," "Game-changing," "Very," "Extremely."
- **Max Length**: Split sentences over 25 words.

## [STRATEGIST] Protocol (PDL Framework)
Every technical story must follow:
1. **Problem**: What was blocking progress?
2. **Decision**: Why did we choose this technical path? (The "Why")
3. **Learning**: What is the reproducible outcome?

## [DESIGNER] Protocol (Aesthetics)
- **Palette**: Solar-Tech (Dark Background, Solar Orange accents, Cyber Cyan details).
- **Empty Chair Audit**: Can a busy CTO understand the value in 5 seconds?
- **Micro-interactions**: Use subtle animations to reinforce quality, not distract.
