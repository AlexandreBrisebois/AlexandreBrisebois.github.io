# Content Spec: Voice & Persona

## 1. Professional Bio (`content/about.md`)
- **Voice**: Reflective-vulnerable blended with urgently excited.
- **Pronouns**: Use "I" for personal learning and opinions; "We" for shared technical outcomes.
- **Content**:
  - Briefly mention the 11+ years at Microsoft as an "origin story" only.
  - Focus on current work at the intersection of multi-cloud (GCP, AWS, Azure), AI agents, and serverless patterns.
  - Reference the "srvrlss.dev" brand as a fresh start.
- **Tone**: Technical Outcome Leader. Senior, authoritative, yet approachable.
- **Formatting**: 3-4 paragraphs max. Point to LinkedIn and GitHub.

## 2. Sample Posts

### Post 1: `hello-world.md`
- **Title**: "Why I Built This Blog"
- **Thematic Goal**: A short, honest reflection on GitOps, plain Markdown, and the anti-CMS manifesto.
- **Voice**: First-person, no fluff. ~300 words.

### Post 2: `first-retrospective.md`
- **Title**: "Lessons Learned: A Project That Went Sideways"
- **Thematic Goal**: An engineering retrospective reframed as a "Lessons Learned" post.
- **Structure**:
  - What happened
  - The Pivot Points
  - Lessons for the Future (Gained Expertise)
- **Features**: Demonstrate the `callout` shortcode. ~500 words.

## 3. Brand Voice Audit (Guardrails)
- **Banned Words**: `Utilize`, `Deep-dive`, `Game-changing`, `Synergy`, `Very`, `Extremely`, `Robust`, `Additionally`, `Furthermore`, `Moreover`.
- **Rhythm**: Long exploratory sentence → short punch → medium reflective.
- **Cadence**: Single-sentence paragraphs as signature moves.
